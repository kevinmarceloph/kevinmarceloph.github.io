Hello World!

Jan 1st, 2015 12:00 am

Hello world! I am Kevin Marcelo, a follower of multiple passions. I previously describe myself as a jack of all trades, but I don’t have a good list of trades to be a jack of. So, in the spirit of “follow your passion,” I follow multiple passions.

I have a lot of ideas waiting to be brought to life, and I’m on a long journey to finally executing them. I am a full stack-ish web developer (Python + Django + basic front end + basic server), and while I try to keep myself updated with new technologies, I care more about picking up the other skills needed to make full products.

Outside ideas and projects, I am also a trope enthusiast. I’ve long thought myself as otaku, gamer, reader and cinemaphile combined (plus some TV series and comic books), but now I understand that what I get from them are the plots and patterns, more known now as tropes. This also explains why I spread myself across many media instead of focusing on one, and also why I play games for their narratives. TV Tropes has ruined my life, and that’s good.

I also care a lot about rationality, both in the sense of getting my beliefs straight and using my knowledge to achieve my goals. (As “rationality” can mean someting completely different to you, I recommend you read my favorite piece of literature, Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality.) I also care about expressing my points with the sharpness of knives, which I cannot reliably do yet, as I’m not a good writer yet in general.

I am also human, so I also want to get fit, cook good food, see the world, and many more (25 of them in total). I probably cannot do all of them, but I want to get as far as I can, ticking off a few a time.
